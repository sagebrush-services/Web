# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

The Luxe project commits to fostering an inclusive environment where all
participants—regardless of age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity,
experience level, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance,
race, religion, or sexual orientation—can engage without harassment.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviors include:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

**Unacceptable behaviors include:**

- Sexualized language or unwelcome sexual attention
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political
  attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic
  address
- Conduct inappropriate in professional settings

## Our Responsibilities

Maintainers must clarify acceptable behavior standards and enforce them
fairly. They may remove, edit, or reject contributions and temporarily or
permanently ban contributors for violations.

## Scope

The code applies within project spaces and when representing the project
publicly (official emails, social media, events).

## Enforcement

Report violations to **<support@neonlaw.org>**. All complaints receive
confidential review and appropriate responses. Maintainers failing to
enforce this code may face repercussions.
